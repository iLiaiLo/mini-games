Try to catch ball

About game

This is a simple game where you try to catch a moving dot by clicking on it.
The game starts when you click the "start" button, and you have 1 minute to score as many points as possible.
The dot will appear in random positions within the frame, and it will disappear after a short time.
you need to click on it to score points.
Each time you click the dot, you earn a point.
You can play the game by clicking the link below.
Good luck!
